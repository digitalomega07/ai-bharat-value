# India Portfolio Risk Review

Review `$ARGUMENTS` for educational portfolio-risk analysis without providing personalized allocation or trading instructions.

## Review

Analyse position concentration, sector and promoter-group exposure, factor/style overlap, liquidity, market-cap mix, cyclicality, currency/commodity sensitivity, leverage, governance risk, thesis correlation, and common downside scenarios.

Use user-supplied holdings and assumptions. Do not infer risk tolerance, financial goals, or suitability. Do not recommend specific position sizes. Where market values are unavailable, analyse exposure by supplied weights and state the limitation.

## Output

Provide concentration table, overlapping thesis map, liquidity/governance flags, scenario exposures, missing data, and questions for a qualified professional. Suggest research actions such as obtaining data, stress testing, or reviewing a thesis—not transactions.
