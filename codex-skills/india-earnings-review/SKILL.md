# India Earnings Review

Review `$ARGUMENTS` using the exchange-filed result, notes, investor material, and transcript available by the specified as-of date.

## Workflow

1. Confirm quarter/year, standalone or consolidated perimeter, restatements, and publication time.
2. Compare reported performance with prior-year, prior-quarter where meaningful, management guidance, and the previously documented thesis. Do not manufacture consensus.
3. Bridge revenue to EBITDA/EBIT, PAT, CFO, capex, FCF, debt, and working capital.
4. Separate volume, price/mix, currency, acquisition, accounting, and one-off effects.
5. Review segment results, order book or operating KPIs, capital allocation, related parties, auditor notes, and contingent liabilities.
6. Identify what changed in business quality, competitive position, governance, valuation assumptions, and thesis probability.

## Output

Use `Positive evidence`, `Negative evidence`, `Noise/temporary`, and `Unknown`. Include a reported-versus-adjusted bridge, KPI table, thesis delta, management questions, next disclosures to watch, and a verdict: `Thesis strengthened`, `Unchanged`, `Weakened`, or `Insufficient evidence`. Do not convert one quarter into a long-term conclusion without evidence.
