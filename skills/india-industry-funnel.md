# India Industry Funnel

Map and screen `$ARGUMENTS` across Indian listed equities using a transparent funnel.

## Process

1. Define the industry boundary, value chain, profit pools, regulation, cyclicality, and as-of date.
2. Build the broad listed universe from exchange/index/industry evidence. Record exclusions.
3. Run sector-appropriate quality, governance, balance-sheet, liquidity, and disclosure screens.
4. Create a shortlist of no more than ten companies with explicit keep/drop reasons.
5. Compare economics, cost position, market share, reinvestment runway, management, valuation, and downside paths.
6. Select up to three complementary candidates for deeper research; do not call them recommendations.

## Bias controls

Check large-cap, survivor, English-language, recent-performance, narrative, and listed-only bias. Mention material private, cooperative, multinational, or unlisted competitors. In backtests use point-in-time constituents and publication dates.

## Output

Provide value-chain map, universe table, funnel statistics, eliminated-company reasons, shortlist comparison, key industry indicators, and three candidates with the next diligence questions. Separate industry attractiveness from stock valuation.
