# India Thesis Tracker

Create or update a falsifiable research thesis for `$ARGUMENTS`.

## Thesis record

Store:

- company, symbols, as-of date, source cutoff, and research owner;
- one-sentence business thesis and one-sentence valuation thesis;
- 3-5 evidence pillars with baseline values and source IDs;
- explicit prove, weaken, and kill conditions;
- expected disclosure dates and monitoring cadence;
- known unknowns, governance watchpoints, and valuation assumptions.

## Update protocol

At each update, preserve the previous wording. Classify changes as:

- `Fact changed`
- `Estimate changed`
- `Valuation changed`
- `Risk changed`
- `Wording changed without new evidence`

Do not rewrite the original thesis to fit subsequent results. Identify thesis drift, moving goalposts, and reliance on price movement as evidence.

## Output

Provide status `Strengthened`, `Intact`, `Watch`, `Impaired`, or `Falsified`; evidence log; trigger dashboard; differences from the prior version; next review date; and unresolved questions. Status is a research conclusion, not personalized trade advice.
